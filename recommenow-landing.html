How it works
Four steps.
One profile that travels.
1
You share a link
Send your unique RecommeNow link to anyone you've worked with — colleagues, managers, clients, vendors, collaborators. No account needed on their end.
2
They fill 60 seconds
A focused form — they state who they are, how they know you, what traits stand out, and write two to four sentences in their own words. Then verify with a work email.
3
Your profile builds
Every vouch stacks into a live reputation profile — a trust score, your top traits ranked by how often they're mentioned, and real quotes from real people with their names attached.
4
It goes everywhere you go
Share a link. Embed a widget on your portfolio. Generate a PDF for your application. Paste a badge into your LinkedIn About. Your reputation travels with you.
